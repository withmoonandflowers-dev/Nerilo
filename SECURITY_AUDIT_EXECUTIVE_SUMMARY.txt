HIGH-PRIORITY FINDINGS
================================================================================

4. NO AUTHENTICATION FALLBACK (Insecure Design)
   - Anonymous users auto-authenticated on app load
   - Should require explicit email/OAuth login
   - Affects: AuthContext.tsx:44-52

5. WEBRTC OFFER/ANSWER NOT SIGNED (Data Integrity)
   - SDP transmitted as plaintext through Firestore
   - Vulnerable to man-in-the-middle attack
   - Affects: P2PConnectionManager.ts:318-350

6. NO SESSION TIMEOUT (Authentication)
   - Users remain authenticated indefinitely
   - Should enforce 30-minute idle timeout
   - Affects: AuthContext.tsx (session management)

7. MISSING RATE LIMITING (DoS/DDoS Risk)
   - Signal collection can be spammed unlimited
   - No per-user rate limits
   - Affects: firestore.rules signals section
